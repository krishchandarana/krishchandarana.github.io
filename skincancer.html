Skin Cancer Detection Model

An AI algorithim to detect benign and malignant skin cancer tumors.

What Are GANs?

Skin cancer is themost common form of cancer humans get.

Skin cancer is mainly detected visually. It detection and treatment starts with clinical screening and then a dermoscopic analysis or biopsy. A dermoscopic analysis is essentially looking at the skin and analyzing it, and a biopsy is the removal of a tissue from the suspected tumor in order to perform further tests on it.
